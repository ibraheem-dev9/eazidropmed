Eazidropmed is a mobile application designed to make medication access **safe, trackable, and pharmacist-aware**, not just delivered.

𝐓𝐡𝐞 𝐏𝐫𝐨𝐛𝐥𝐞𝐦
Access to medication in Nigeria is risky and fragmented:

- ❌ Fake and unverified drugs  
- ❌ No structured prescription handling  
- ❌ No visibility into delivery progress  
- ❌ High risk of misuse and wrong medication  

Most platforms only focus on 𝐝𝐞𝐥𝐢𝐯𝐞𝐫𝐲, not 𝐬𝐚𝐟𝐞𝐭𝐲.

𝐓𝐡𝐞 𝐒𝐨𝐥𝐮𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧
Eazidropmed introduces a 𝐬𝐚𝐟𝐞𝐭𝐲-𝐟𝐢𝐫𝐬𝐭 𝐦𝐞𝐝𝐢𝐜𝐚𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧 𝐬𝐲𝐬𝐭𝐞𝐦.

Users can:
- 📦 Order medications easily  
- 📄 Upload prescriptions for verification  
- 📍 Track orders in real-time  
- 💬 Contact support instantly  

Admins can:
- 📋 Review all orders  
- 📎 View prescriptions before approval  
- 🔄 Update delivery status  
- 📞 Contact customers directly (call / WhatsApp)

𝐀𝐝𝐦𝐢𝐧 𝐒𝐢𝐝𝐞
